Scholarship Process

The #YesWeCode Fund represents a unique partnership across organizations providing technology skills-based education. Students will follow the admissions process for the school of their choosing. Once accepted to the school, they can apply for the scholarship.

Find the #YesWeCode Fund

Students find out about the opportunity through:

  • Through the schools participating in #YesWeCode Fund
  • Through the #YesWeCode website
  • The press or public outlets

Students Apply to A Partner School

Students who apply will need to complete the program application process and be accepted to a Fund partner school. This:

  • allows for each school to be compliant with their processes and state admissions regulations
  • ensures that incoming students will benefit from the respective program and are set up for success

Apply to the #YesWeCode Fund

Once the participating school accepts each student, they will provide the application to apply for the scholarship through Climb Credit's application.

  • Person of color or identify as female
  • Be accepted to their school of choice
  • Meet the financial qualifications

    • Learn more about diversity in high-tech

Verification Approval

Climb Credit will verify the financial need of the student. Once Climb Credit verifies that the student qualifies, the students are notified by the School that they application to the #YesWeCode Fund was accepted and approved.

Scholarship Awarded

Congrats! Once the scholarship is awarded, the students will be able to start and complete programs and receive assistance through their respective schools and career support programs.
